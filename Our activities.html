Embrace Your Health

HIV Awareness Education: Conducted sessions stressing the benefits of knowing one's HIV status, the importance of regular testing, and where to find free and confidential HIV testing services.

HIV Testing Campaign: Used 400 self-test kits, assisting 48 participants in using the tests. One positive case was identified in a 25-year-old pregnant female, who was counseled and referred for further care.

In collaboration with REAP and Musha Health Center, we conducted the "Get Tested, Stay Healthy" campaign at Rwamagana district, Musha sector, providing HIV/AIDS awareness and free testing.
