Get Tested, Stay Healthy

In collaboration with REAP and Musha Health Center, we conducted the "Get Tested, Stay Healthy" campaign in Rwamagana District, Musha Sector. This initiative focused on HIV/AIDS awareness and provided free testing for at-risk populations. The goal was to dispel misconceptions about HIV, reduce stigma and discrimination, and encourage testing to prevent further transmission, especially among young adults.

IFG Marks International Day of Peace

"IFG" celebrated International Day of Peace at Rwanda's Parliament, emphasizing "Unity and Resilience for Peace." The event brought together diverse participants to promote peace and unity, highlighting the importance of collective efforts in building peaceful societies and overcoming challenges for a future of lasting peace and prosperity.

Cervical Cancer Awareness

We organized a successful cervical cancer awareness campaign at Apapece Les Gazelles Primary School in Ngoma District. The campaign aimed to educate the community about cervical cancer and emphasized the importance of vaccination and regular screenings in safeguarding the health of future generations.
